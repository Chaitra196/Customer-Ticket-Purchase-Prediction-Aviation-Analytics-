# ✈️ Customer Ticket Purchase Prediction (Aviation Analytics)  
### 🚀 Optimizing marketing campaigns using predictive modeling

---

## 📌 Overview  
This project focuses on predicting whether a customer is likely to purchase an airline ticket based on their online behavior and engagement patterns.  

Two separate models are developed for **laptop users and mobile users** to capture device-specific behavior and improve prediction accuracy.

---

## 🎯 Objective  
- Predict customer ticket purchase propensity  
- Build separate models for mobile and laptop users  
- Optimize digital marketing campaigns  
- Reduce advertising costs and improve ROI  

---

## 📂 Dataset Features  

- **UserID** – Unique user identifier  
- **Buy_ticket** – Target variable (purchase or not)  
- **Yearly_avg_view_on_travel_page** – Travel page engagement  
- **preferred_device** – Device type (mobile/laptop)  
- **total_likes_on_outstation_checkin_given/received** – Social engagement  
- **yearly_avg_Outstation_checkins** – Travel frequency  
- **member_in_family** – Family size  
- **preferred_location_type** – Travel preference  
- **Yearly_avg_comment_on_travel_page** – Interaction level  
- **week_since_last_outstation_checkin** – Recency  
- **following_company_page** – Brand engagement  
- **montly_avg_comment_on_company_page** – Company interaction  
- **working_flag / Adult_flag** – Demographics  
- **travelling_network_rating** – Social influence  
- **Daily_Avg_mins_spend_on_traveling_page** – Time spent
